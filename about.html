**About ROMERO's PIZZA

ROMERO's PIZZA is a family owned pizza resturent featuring hand-tossed italy atyle pizza,chicken wings and toasted subs.The restaurant will offer a dine-in buffet, carryout and delivery to the surrounding neighborhoods and area businesses. Name of the restaurant is for its owner ROMERO, will emulate the family owned Pizzas.In fact, the signature crust and mouthwatering sauce featured at ROMERO's are from family recipes that have been passed down from generation to generation. The decorations for ROMERO's Pizza will feature wood accented construction adorned with paintings and props blended with an Italian theme decor.
